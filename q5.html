Jemmy's letter to the King

Dear King, I would like it if you try to tell royal households from keeping whipping boys. When a whipping boy gets whipped the Prince does not get hurt so he does not learn the lesson and does it again! I say that you should whip the Prince if he is the one who does the bad thing. Also, the Prince has learned a lot. I do not think that the Prince needs a whipping boy. From Jemmy.
