Prince Brat and Jemmy(The whipping boy) at the beginning of the story

JEMMY is smart, brave, and he is very resilient. He lived in the sewers before the King chose him to be the Prince’s whipping boy. Jemmy knows the sewers from top to bottom and he can hide and catch rats in the sewer. He is not afraid when they get caught by Hold-Your-Nose-billy and cutwater but Prince Brat tells cutwater where Jemmy is hiding. PRINCE BRAT is very dumb, meek, and he can’t stand a piece of bread with herring. He is very spoiled. Prince Brat loves getting Jemmy whipped for all the bad things that he does, and Brat does a lot of bad things. He grew up in the castle and is used to giant feasts. He is never whipped so he is very bad.
